Common questions

How do I delete my account?
Open RiddleRush → Settings → Delete Account.

How do I restore purchases?
Open RiddleRush → Settings → Restore Purchases (under the subscription section).

How do I manage my subscription?
Open your device Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → RiddleRush.
